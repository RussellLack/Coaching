AI Navigation Brief excerpt

Example for an Operator profile. Enough to judge usefulness, with the detailed action plan reserved for the paid scan.

Role pattern Operator under strain: coordination value is exposed, judgement value is defensible.

Locked next step Detailed 30-day experiment: redesign one recurring operating meeting into an AI-supported decision process.
Recommended route Workflow & Delegation first, then Relevance & Impact

Sample output tool

Human / AI Decision Boundary

A practical tool used to decide what to automate, augment, protect or treat as high-value human work.

Automate Status synthesis Low judgement, repeatable, high frequency.
Augment Scenario comparison AI drafts options; leader chooses trade-offs.
Protect Stakeholder judgement Context, trust and timing remain human-led.
High-value human work Strategic narrative Turn experience into a memorable point of view.
Decision criteria
  • How much judgement is required?
  • What happens if the decision is wrong?
  • Does this create trust or just move information?
